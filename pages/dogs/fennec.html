About Fennec

Meet Fennec, affectionately known as Fenny by his foster family. This gorgeous boy is approximately 2- 3 years old and loves to play, especially tug! He also loves bones and chew toys. We believe he may be a Pakistani Bull Terrier, or perhaps a Bull Terrier mix.

When Fennec is playing he can get very excited and it’s probably best for him to be with older children only. There are boys, ages 7 and 11 in his foster family. In his excitement he could knock a small child down and hurt them accidentally.

Fennec is deaf, a genetic defect. He’s learning hand signals, and knows sit, down and shake a paw. It is highly recommended that he be taken to obedience for both training and bonding time. Fennec has a fair amount of energy and would do best with an active home who will engage him in agility, jogging or similar to burn this energy.

If you are interested in learning more about this wonderful boy, please fill out an application. After it has been approved we would then have you meet the dog your most interested in.

The adoption fee is $400. Adopters will receive a $50 refund after we receive proof that they have graduated from a min of one level in obedience classes within 6 months of adoption.
